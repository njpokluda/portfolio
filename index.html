About Nick

I turn marketing data into strategic decisions.

My background spans over a decade at a regional financial institution, where I grew from a hybrid marketing and IT role into leading marketing analytics and digital strategy. Along the way, I developed a sharp focus on connecting data to decisions.

I'm drawn to the work of making complex information clear and actionable — whether that's building a dashboard, designing a segmentation strategy, or restructuring a website to improve user experience.
