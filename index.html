About Nick

I turn marketing data into strategic decisions.

Based in Austin, TX, I focus on the intersection of analytics, digital strategy, and customer experience — translating complex data into clear direction for marketing teams and leadership.

My work spans dashboards, segmentation, web experience, and the marketing technology that ties it all together. I care about clarity, and I'm happiest when ideas are backed by evidence.
